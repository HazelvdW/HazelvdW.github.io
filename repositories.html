Basslines-Bodies-BeehiveHaircuts

Favourite Qualitative CC0

Examining the qualitative contents of participants' descriptions of music-influenced mental content (MIMC) while listening to music from 10 genres. Rigorous mixed-method qualitative analysis of 14,099 descriptions — codebook, data, stimuli, and co-occurrence network analysis included.

10 genres 14,099 descriptions Thematic analysis Co-occurrence networks

context-framed-listening

Python NLP

Computational text analysis pipeline for my current paper on context-framed listening. Dual-model BERT approach (BERT-base-uncased + BERTweet) for music-evoked thought (MET) analysis, alongside TF-IDF and Word2Vec, operating at both document and individual MET levels.

BERT BERTweet Word2Vec TF-IDF Semantic similarity

MUSIFEAST-17

Published Jupyter R CC0

356 instrumental 30-second clips across 17 genres, with normative data from 701 UK & US participants (ages 18–75). Ratings of familiarity, enjoyment, emotional expression, genre recognition, and evoked thought types. Includes a Shiny/Plotly app for interactive exploration of the stimulus set.

17 genres 356 stimuli 701 participants Shiny app Behavior Research Methods 2025
