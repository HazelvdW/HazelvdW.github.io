Hi, I'm Hazel

music, imagination, and a wee pinch of wonder

I'm currently a PhD researcher in music psychology at Durham University, exploring what happens in people's mind when listening to music — the images, stories, memories, and strange inner worlds that music influences, shapes, and colours. I currently think of myself as an imagination cartographer, mapping the thoughtscapes of the mind that music can mould. Equal parts scientist and dreamer.
